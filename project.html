Project on Gravity Power Generation

The main concept of this project is to generate power using gravity as our main resource. As it is abundant in nature and cost efficient. Here we have made an arrangement of variable distance,variable angle and variable weight which can be called as DAW Arrangement.The flow of energy in the gravitator. Initially, starting torque is supplied through a permanent magnet DC Motor. The DC motor takes electrical energy and converts it into mechanical rotations of high torque. The DC motor output is given to the DAW arrangement The DAW arrangement converts the potential energy of the weights into rotational kinetic energy. The mechanical rotations are of low speed and high torque. But the DC generator requires high rotations to generate electric power. So, the output of the DAW arrangement is given to the transmission system which converts it accordingly for the DC generator. The DC generator converts the input mechanical energy into electrical energy

Advantages :

  • Gravitational energy has been utilized by the developed mechanism to convert kinetic energy for generation of power. It has the advantages of using gravity as energy source which is the future of power generation.
  • It is eco-friendly as well as freely available.
  • It has been found that gravitator has combined advantages of both Conventional and renewable energy sources.
  • The proposed solution is a unique method for production of electricity
